Old Fashion Recipe

A Classic whiskey cocktail everyone should know how to make.

Ingredients

  • Ice cubes
  • 60 ml of Whiskey
  • Angostrua bitters
  • Orange twist for garnish
  • Sugar cube
  • Cocktail shaker

Steps

  1. Put 2 dashes of bitters over a sugar cube and muddle it in a chilled rocks glass
  2. Add a couple of ice cubes and leave it to chill for 30 seconds
  3. Add your whiskey
  4. Stir add your orange twist garnish to add some presentation to the drink
  5. Enjoy!
